NBA Team's Rely More on Three Pointers Than Ever Before

By Brendan Weissel

In the past 25 years the NBA has seen a slow rise in the number of threes attempted per game. In 2025 that number is at its highest ever at 37.5 three pointers attempted per game.

From 2001 to 2024 the way teams rely on three pointers has changed. In 2001, 15.3 percent of scoring was from beyond the arc. In 2024 that number is more than doubled.

Despite the revolution in game style in the past 25 years in the NBA, the number of free throws attmepted per game has remained stable.
